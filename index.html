Welcome to The Ribeira Sacra

Hiking in The Ribeira Sacra

Due to its steep cliffs, bays and lush greenery, Galicia is a great attraction for those who love hiking.

The Galician estuaries (long arms of the sea that enter the land) prevail in the passage. The view of the Sil river canyon from the Balcones de Madrid viewpoint is an example of the beauties to be found.

To discover this fantastic landscape on foot, you will find everything from lonely paths to busy roads, among which is the most famous pilgrimage route: the Camino de Santiago.

Biking in The Ribeira Sacra

These routes should not be considered a normal hiking trail, with signs and a definite route.

These are long-distance routes, with a significant element of adventure, for which you will have to plan the logistics for your journey and will find all kinds of paths, tracks and trails.

They are perfect for travelling for several days by bike or hiking and getting to know the most interesting places in the Ribeira Sacra.
