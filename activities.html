The Mino Route

Directions

The route is divided into two very different sections. The two paths that go down from A Peroxa to Gulfariz and the one that follows the banks the banks of the R. Mino from Gulfariz to Os Peares. From Ourense take the N-120 in the direction of Monforte, take the Os Peares exit and continue to the village, where the river beach is located.

Stats

  • Length: 11km
  • Difficulty: Medium
  • Duration: 3 hours 30 minutes
  • Bike Access: Unsuitable
  • Services: Restaurents and accommodation in A Peroxa and Os Peares and health centre in Os Peares.
